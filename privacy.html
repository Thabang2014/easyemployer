Privacy Policy

EasyEmploy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information Collection

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as your name, email address, and resume details.

Information Use

We use your information to provide and improve our services, to communicate with you, and to match you with job opportunities.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website.
